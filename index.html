Sheila's your bot for all of your Discord needs

Sheila is an instance of Red with custom forks of most common features you'd use. Here's a highlight of some of her features:

Leveling

Reward your server members by having ranks for chatting, and even have give roles when they hit a milestone.

Economy

Claim money, gamble money, pay people, compete. Setup your own server economy and let your members compete.

Music

Can't get any music lately? Sheila's got your back. Stream music from Soundcloud, Spotify, or YouTube in VC.

Puns

Ever need a joke, even if you didn't necceserially mean to ask for it at the moment? Sheila's got just that thing.

Moderation

Need a seamless moderation tool? Sheila covers mutes, bans, kicks, warnings, and records them in moderation log cases.

Highlight

Want to know when someone mentions you, but doesn't ping you? Set a list of keywords and Sheila will DM you when they are said!
